Chocolate Lava Cake

Description

Small chocolate cake with a center that is rich and melty.

Ingredients

  • Cooking oil
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 2 ounces of bittersweet chocolate
  • 2 ounces of semisweet chocolate
  • 1 1/4 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Cover baking cups with oil and place on baking sheet.
  2. Melt butter, bittersweet chocolate, and semisweet chocolate in the microwave for 1 minute. Wisk until completely melted. Wisk in sugar, eggs, egg yolks, vanilla, and four.
  3. Pour in baking cups.
  4. Bake until the sides are firm and the center is soft. Should be about 13 minutes. Lets stand for 1 minute.
