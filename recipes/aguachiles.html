Aguachiles

Description

Similar to ceviche, Aguachiles is a dish made of shrimp marinated in lime juce with a mix of vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound raw shrimp
  • 4 large limes
  • salt
  • 1/4 of a red onion
  • 1 garlic clove
  • cilantro
  • 2 jalapenos
  • cucumber
  • tortilla chips or tostadas

Steps

  1. Remove any veins or tails from the shrimp. Place in a serving dish. Squeeze lime to cover the shrimp and sprinkle with salt.
  2. Cook shrimp on both sides in the lime juice for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Slice red onion and season with salt. Cover the onions with water.
  1. To make the marinade, place 1/2 cup of lime juice, 1 garlic clove, 1 cup of cilantro, 2 sliced jalapenos, and salt in a blender.
  2. Pour marinade over the shrimp. Scatter onions on top. Add cucumbers.
  3. Refrigerate 30 minutes or up to 4 hours.
  4. Enjoy!
